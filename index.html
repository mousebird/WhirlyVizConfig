WhirlyViz URL Examples

WhirlyViz does a lot of complex, pretty things, but it'll also do a couple of simple things if you ask nicely.

If you feed WhirlyViz a URL (on the device) it'll load a basemap and some number of GeoJSON files. Navigate to this page on your iPhone or iPad and then tap on one of the links.
