Instructions

  • Tap and drag to pan
  • Pinch to zoom
  • Tap Edit in the upper right to change the selected routes and dates
  • Tap a stop to see specific values.
  • Tap outside a stop to clear.

San Francisco Transit Data

The data set was generated for the 2012 Urban Data Challenge. It consists of bus passenger counts over a subset of San Francisco buses for the week of October 1 2012.
