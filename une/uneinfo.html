Instructions

  • Tap and drag to pan
  • Pinch to zoom
  • Tap to select one or more countries.
  • Tap again to see a specific data value.
  • Select Edit in the upper right to change the data set.
  • Tap outside the globe to clear.

UNEP Data

The data sets are taken from the United Nations Environment Programme web site and represent a subset of available country statistics.
