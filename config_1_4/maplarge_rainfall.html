Instructions

  • Tap and drag to pan
  • Pinch to zoom
  • Tap Edit in the upper right to change between states, counties, and zip codes.
  • Tap and release to see the data value at a specific point.

Maplarge rainfall example

This visualization shows the rainfall over the USA leading up to Dec 15 2011. More rain is shown in darker colors and less in lighter. You can tap to see a specific value.
